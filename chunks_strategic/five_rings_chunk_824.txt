She had many husbands, and caused the ruin of many a powerful man. Because of her ambition to be a dancer she “accidentally” ran into Alexandre Dujarier, then the owner of France’s most popular newspaper and a drama critic himself. Although still legally married to an Englishman with whom she eloped at 19, Lola kept her status secret from all her husbands.
